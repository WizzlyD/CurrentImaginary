01 / Our Story

We are Current Imaginary, a creative division under PT. Grup Meraki Indonesia.

a company established in Jakarta, Indonesia. We have been engaged in the creative industry since 2016. Over the years, we have delivered numerous projects for both government and corporate clients.

Our primary focus is on visual production for MICE (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions).events and other design-driven digital content. We are a collective of makers, dreamers, and doers—fueled by love and creative ambition. With our passionate strengths and heart in the art, we believe that every need will anchor in the right place.
